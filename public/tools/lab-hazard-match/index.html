Reference summary / 參考摘要

Full bilingual notes for all fourteen laboratory apparatus and seven OSHC hazard labels — use for DSE practical language revision.

HKDSE lab revision: fourteen laboratory apparatus images plus seven OSHC «化學品標籤» hazard labels. On real chemicals follow the container label and your school rules.

How to play

Flip two cards each turn. One card shows the name in English and 中文; the other shows a picture of the same item — either a laboratory apparatus image or one of the seven hazard labels from the Hong Kong OSHC «化學品標籤 CHEMICAL LABELS» poster. Match every label to its picture in as few moves and as little time as you can.

  • 量度Measurement — measuring cylinder, volumetric flask, burette, pipette (4 pairs).
  • 容器Containers & reaction — beaker, conical flask, test tube, test tube rack, round-bottom flask, Bunsen burner, stirring rod (7 pairs).
  • 分離Separation — funnel, evaporating dish, watch glass (3 pairs).
  • 危險Hazard (OSHC poster) — seven classes: 爆炸性、助燃、易燃、腐蝕性、刺激性、有毒、有害. Label cards show a colour band, a mini label image, and a recognition hint. Picture cards show the full poster label. Use Symbol guide before you play.
  • Mixed shuffles glassware + hazard pairs together for a longer deck.
  • Hint (+5) briefly highlights one full pair still face-down; it adds 5 to your move count.
  • Scroll to the Reference summary below the board (or click Reference in the header) for full bilingual notes on every apparatus and hazard label.

Hazard symbol guide (OSHC poster)

Match the colour band and black pictogram to the English / 中文 name. Yellow = explosion or oxidizing; red = flammable; white = health / corrosion risks.
